About me

MY NAME IS JOSE GARCIA. I AM A FREELANCE AND PUBLISHED PHOTOGRAPHER BASED IN LOS ANGELES ,CA

I fell in love with photography since I was a child. Growing up my mom bought me a camera and I would love to go out and explore and take photographs. In all the family gatherings I would make sure to capture all the magical moments. It is amazing how with the press of a button I can freeze an expression or even a feeling.

My passion for photography has led me to take all types of images. I have worked in the areas of fitness, fashion, sports, family events, weddings, sweet 16, quinceaneras, and all kinds of photography. It makes me happy to help capture those magical moments. I also love to do help out in the community by doing volunteering in events like Tour De Cure, AAD, LA Chinatown Fire Cracker, and other types of community events.
